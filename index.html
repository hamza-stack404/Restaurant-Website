Our Story

One hearth, one fire, never switched off since the day we opened.

Ember & Salt began with a simple idea: strip the kitchen down to its oldest tool. No gas lines, no induction — every dish that leaves our pass has touched the same wood fire, tended around the clock by our team of fire-keepers.

We work with small fishing crews along the coast and a handful of growers further inland, building the menu around what arrives that morning rather than what a printed menu promises.
